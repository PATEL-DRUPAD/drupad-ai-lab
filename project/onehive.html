Featured case study

OneHive

Knowledge platform powered by Retrieval-Augmented Generation for organized, searchable intelligence.

Problem

Knowledge work becomes slow when useful context is scattered across documents, notes, and disconnected tools.

System Direction

OneHive organizes content into a retrieval-first knowledge layer that can support search, summarization, and assistant-style workflows.

Engineering Focus

Information architecture, retrieval design, answer quality, and clean interfaces for exploring knowledge.
